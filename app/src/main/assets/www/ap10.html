The MBA programme at Cambridge Institute of Technology Bangalore is an attempt to build people skills for the students with focus on team skills, indispensable to any organization. A comprehensive teaching, learning pedagogy including real life situations coupled with management games, mock drill, presentations by students, Group Discussions and case studies are used to chisel the best out of the budding manager and business leader. As a multi-disciplinary business school we offer a full portfolio of business, with emphasis in Marketing, Finance and Human Resource Management. Majority of our highly qualified, dedicated and committed faculty members have years of experience in teaching and the industry.

To enhance learning, our faculty employs a variety of teaching methods, including case analysis, individual and group projects, simulations, classroom discussions, guest lectures and active learning modules. The beautiful Cambridge campus with its new block and state of art facilities provide an ideal environment for students to develop professionally..
