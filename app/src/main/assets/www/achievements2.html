Vocational Training Programmes

Cambridge Institute of Technology organizes vocational training programmes for all semesters in the Months of June/ July and Dec/Jan. The certification courses enables the students to stand forefront in their career and also make them industry ready. The courses conducted were Foreign Languages (French / German), C Programming, C++ Programming, MAT Lab & its Application, Advance C Programming, Web Content Development using J2EE, Oracle SQL 11g, Cloud Infrastructure Management, Infosys Campus Connect, CATIA, OSCAD Training.

The Management and Principal would like to congratulate both the faculty on successful completion of the courses and the students for their active participation.
